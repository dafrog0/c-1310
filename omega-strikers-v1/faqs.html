FAQs

What kind of game is Omega Strikers?
Omega Strikers is a brand new knockout striker with a cast of characters that come from a bright and hopefully futuristic society. These characters will engage in 3v3 ranked and 3v3 unranked combat, with a variety of modes to come in the future
Who is developing Omega Strikers?
Omega Strikers is developed by Odyssey Interactive.
Who is Publishing Omega Strikers
Omega Strikers is self published by Odyssey Interactive.
When does Omega Strikers release
Omega Strikers release into closed beta on September 16th, 2022, and will transition to open beta shortly after.
How much does Omega Strikers cost
Omega Strikers will be free-to-play supported by optional in game purchases such as cosmetics, characters, and a seasonal battle pass. Omega Strikers will NOT be a pay for power game.
What platforms is Omega Strikers available on
Omega Strikers will be available on PC through Steam, and in the future will be available on iOS and Android mobile devices, as well as consoles in the future.
Will Omega Strikers have cross-play and cross-progression.
Yes Omega Strikers will support cross-play and cross-progression once it is available on multiple platforms.
How many characters will be in Omega Strikers
Our launch roster will have 10 characters, with an additional 5 released over the first season. Every season will include character drops into the foreseeable future.
Will Omega Strikers have a competitive scene
Odyssey will support a competitive scene if the community is large enough and is hungry for one. We don't believe in shoehorning a competitive ecosystem into a game, and would rather work with players on building a scene that makes sense for them and the title.
Is Omega Strikers a Web3 game and will you integrate NFTs or Blockchain technology.
No, we're building games the old fashioned way with no plans to integrate either NFTs or web3 technology.
